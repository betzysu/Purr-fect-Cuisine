Million Dollar Chicken Casserole

Are you having the family over for dinner, Thanksgiving, or Christmas and you're running out of time? Well, look no further! Cook up this delicious Million Dollar Chicken Casserole! Your friends and family are guaranteed to enjoy this cheesy, creamy, and baked dish.

prep time

10 mins

cook time

30 mins

serving

6 servings

instructions

step 1

Preheat oven to 350°F (180°C)

step 2

Lightly spray a 9×9-inch baking dish with cooking spray and set aside.

step 3

Combine chicken, cream cheese, cottage cheese, sour cream, garlic powder, onion powder, and cream of chicken soup. Spread into prepared pan.

step 4

Combine crushed crackers and butter, sprinkle over chicken mixture.

step 5

Bake uncovered 30 to 40 minutes.

step 6

Enjoy!

ingredients

4 ½ cups chopped cooked chicken

4 oz cream cheese, room temperature

1 cup cottage cheese

½ cup sour cream

1 (10.75-oz) can cream of chicken soup

¼ tsp garlic powder

¼ tsp dried onion powder

1 sleeve Ritz crackers, crushed

5 Tbsp butter, melted

tools

9x9 Baking Dish

Spatula

Measuring Spoons

Measuring Cups
