Vegan Mushroom Stroganoff

Instead of whipping up Beef Stroganoff for dinner, try out this vegan Mushroom Stroganoff! You'll save yourself the calories and have some extra vitamin B! This dish is great with pasta, mashed potatoes, or even with rice. Add in portobello mushrooms for a robust flavor!

prep time

8 mins

cook time

12 mins

serving

4 servings

instructions

step 1

Finely chop the onion. Heat some oil in a large pan and saut√© the onion for 2-3 minutes until they're translucent. Add the garlic and cook it for another minute.

step 2

Add the flour, the tomato paste, and the paprika powder and stir well. Cook for 2 minutes until lightly brown.

step 3

Deglaze with white wine and simmer for a minute.

step 4

Then add the mushrooms and stir well with a wooden spoon so they're covered with the tomato paste onion mixture. Cook on high heat for 5 minutes.

step 5

Add the vegan sour cream. Season with freshly pressed lemon juice, salt, and pepper. Serve immediately over pasta or mashed potatoes and sprinkle with freshly chopped parsley.

ingredients

1 onion, chopped

2 cloves of garlic, minced

1 tablespoon tomato paste

2 teaspoons all-purpose flour

1/4 cup white wine

1 teaspoon paprika powder

6 cups brown button mushrooms

1/2 cup shiitake mushrooms

3 teaspoons vegan sour cream

1 teaspoon lemon juice

salt, to taste

black pepper, to taste

freshly chopped parsley
