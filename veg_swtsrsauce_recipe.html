Vegan Sweet and Sour Stir Fry Sauce

This sweet and stir fry sauce is easy, quick, and yummy! It is also universal! Use it as a dip for your eggrolls, a sauce for your chicken over white rice, or simply for your vegetables. The choices are endless!

prep time

5 mins

cook time

10 mins

serving

1/2 cup serving

instructions

step 1

Stir together water and cornstarch until combined.

step 2

In a small pot, whisk together sugar, vinegar, water, soy sauce and ketchup until combined.

step 3

Add the corn starch and water and stir. Bring to a simmer on medium heat and whisk until it thickens. This should only take a few minutes.

step 4

Enjoy! Best served as a stir fry sauce. Add in some pineapple for extra tangy sweetness!

ingredients

¼ cup cane sugar

2 tablespoons vinegar

¼ cup water

2 tablespoons soy sauce

1 tablespoon ketchup

1 tablespoon cornstarch + 1 tablespoon water mixed together

tools

Mixing Bowls

Whisk

Measuring Spoons

Measuring Cups
